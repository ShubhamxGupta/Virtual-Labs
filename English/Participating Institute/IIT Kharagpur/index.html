IIT Kharagpur

Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur) is one of India's most prestigious and oldest IITs, established in 1951 as the first in the IIT system. Known for its excellence in education, research, and innovation, IIT Kharagpur offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across engineering, sciences, management, law, and humanities. The institute is renowned for its cutting-edge research in areas such as artificial intelligence, robotics, sustainable energy, and healthcare technology. With a sprawling 2,100-acre campus, IIT Kharagpur boasts world-class infrastructure, modern laboratories, and vibrant student life. It has a strong entrepreneurial culture, fostering startups and industry collaborations through initiatives like the Technology Incubation and Entrepreneurship Training Society (TIETS). IIT Kharagpur continues to play a crucial role in shaping India's technological and economic landscape through its contributions to innovation and research.
