IIT Guwahati

Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT Guwahati) is one of India's leading technical institutions, known for its excellence in education, research, and innovation. Established in 1994, it is the sixth member of the prestigious IIT system and is situated on the banks of the Brahmaputra River in Assam. The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, science, and humanities, fostering interdisciplinary research and technological advancements. IIT Guwahati is recognized for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, world-class faculty, and strong industry collaborations. It is also home to several research centers focusing on emerging fields like artificial intelligence, nanotechnology, and sustainable energy. With a vibrant campus life and a strong entrepreneurial culture, IIT Guwahati continues to contribute significantly to scientific and technological progress in India and beyond.
