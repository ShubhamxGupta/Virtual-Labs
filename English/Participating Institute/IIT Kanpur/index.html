IIT Kanpur

Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur) is one of India's premier engineering institutions, renowned for its excellence in education, research, and technological innovation. Established in 1959 with assistance from a consortium of U.S. universities, IIT Kanpur has played a crucial role in shaping India's scientific and technological landscape. The institute offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, sciences, management, and humanities. It is known for its cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence, aerospace engineering, robotics, and sustainable energy. IIT Kanpur has a strong entrepreneurial ecosystem, fostering startups and industry collaborations through its Startup Incubation and Innovation Centre (SIIC). The campus, spread over 1,055 acres, provides world-class infrastructure, modern laboratories, and a vibrant student community. With a commitment to innovation and societal impact, IIT Kanpur continues to be a leader in academic and technological advancements both in India and globally.
