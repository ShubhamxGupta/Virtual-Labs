IIT Bombay

Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) is one of India's premier engineering and research institutions, renowned globally for its academic excellence and innovation. Established in 1958 with the support of UNESCO and the Soviet Union, IIT Bombay has since become a hub for cutting-edge research, entrepreneurship, and technological advancements. The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, sciences, design, management, and humanities. With a strong emphasis on interdisciplinary research, IIT Bombay has contributed significantly to fields such as artificial intelligence, nanotechnology, sustainable energy, and biotechnology. The campus, located in Powai, Mumbai, is known for its vibrant student life, world-class infrastructure, and strong industry collaborations. IIT Bombay also fosters startups and innovation through initiatives like the Society for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (SINE). It continues to be a leader in shaping India's technological landscape and producing world-class professionals.
